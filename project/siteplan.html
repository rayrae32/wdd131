Website Plan for EcoTravel Adventures

Site Name

EcoTravel Adventures

This name represents a website focused on providing sustainable travel tips and guides for eco-friendly destinations around the world.

Optional domain availability: ecotraveladventures.org

Site Purpose

The site aims to promote sustainable travel by providing detailed guides, tips for eco-friendly practices, and recommendations for green destinations. It will also include a blog and a community forum for travelers to share their experiences.

Scenarios

  • What are the top eco-friendly destinations to visit this year?
  • How can I reduce my carbon footprint while traveling?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - used for headings and buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (White) - used for background and text.
  • Accent Color: #FFD700 (Gold) - used for highlights and accents.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
